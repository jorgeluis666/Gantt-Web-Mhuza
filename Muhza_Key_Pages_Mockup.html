The Cycle — education hub

Goal: own the concept. Static preview below — the interactive version (clickable circle + scroll story) is in the separate "Cycle Hub Interactive" file.
The science, simply

Your skin doesn't stay the same all month. Your routine shouldn't either.

Estrogen and progesterone rise and fall across your cycle — changing oil production, hydration, and sensitivity. Cycle-synced skincare meets skin where it is, in each phase.

Menstrual

Days 1–5

Hormones bottom out. Skin is drier, duller, and more sensitive.

Focus: gentle hydration + barrier support → Ilari Oil Cleanser

Follicular

Days 6–13

Estrogen rises; collagen and glow build. Skin is resilient and bright.

Focus: brighten & renew → active treatments tolerate well here

Ovulatory

~Day 14

Estrogen peaks — skin glows, but oil starts to climb.

Focus: keep it balanced & lightweight

Luteal

Days 15–28

Progesterone rises; oil and breakouts are most likely.

Focus: clarify, calm & protect → Muyu Bundle
